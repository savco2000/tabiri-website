Company Overview

Tabiri Analytics, Inc. is a small business delivering secure AI automation, cybersecurity monitoring, and data intelligence solutions for organizations that need operational efficiency without compromising security.

We build domain-specific AI agents, automated workflows, and resilient cybersecurity infrastructure designed for sensitive, high-compliance environments.

Past Performance

CDP Engineers / MapSync (Geospatial Mapping and Infrastructure, United States) - Ongoing enterprise cybersecurity monitoring protecting critical GPS/GIS data systems utilized by US local governments. Engineered automated telemetry logging and vulnerability mapping to safeguard vital municipal infrastructure networks.

Elijah's Bridge Initiative (Federal Legal Services and Systems Navigation, United States) - Scaled a validated single-matter assistant into a secure, multi-tenant GCP platform running an autonomous OpenClaw agent fleet. Implemented cryptographic multi-tenancy via Postgres Row-Level Security (RLS) and Cloud KMS customer-managed keys to isolate privileged records. Ingested and processed over 500 complex documents, transcribed 500+ audio minutes via secure API protocols, and cross-referenced thousands of legal citations with 100% precision. Enforced an immutable human-gated workflow that compressed weeks of manual review down to machine-speed hours while completely neutralizing data hallucination risks.
